About the Liberation War Archive

About the Liberation War Archive The Liberation War Archive is a dedicated digital repository preserving the historical significance of Bangladesh’s Liberation War of 1971. It serves as an invaluable resource for researchers, historians, and the general public, offering a comprehensive collection of documents, interviews, photographs, and multimedia content that narrate the struggles, sacrifices, and triumphs of the war. Our Mission To preserve and document the rich history of the Liberation War. To provide authentic, verifiable resources for researchers and students. To honor the freedom fighters, martyrs, and victims who played a crucial role in Bangladesh’s independence. What You’ll Find in the Archive Historical Documents: Official treaties, declarations, and correspondence.
